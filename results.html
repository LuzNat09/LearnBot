Volcanoes are like Earth's giant chimneys!

Way deep down inside our planet, it's so hot that even rocks melt! This hot, gooey rock is called magma.

When the pressure gets too much, it bursts out through a crack in the ground. Once it's outside, we call it lava. It's like a glowing orange river!
